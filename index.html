Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our process and services.

How long does it take?
It depends on the package you choose. Our Starter sites take 3 days, while our most complex Elite packages are delivered within 10 days.
Do I need to provide content?
Ideally, yes. However, if you're struggling, we can help with basic copywriting and provide high-quality stock images to get you started.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cryptocurrency payments only, processed securely through MaxelPay. After we agree on a package, you'll receive a crypto invoice via MaxelPay. We accept Bitcoin, USDT, and other major cryptocurrencies. Fast, secure, and borderless..
Can I update the site myself?
Yes! We build our sites on user-friendly platforms that allow you to easily edit text and images without any coding knowledge.
Do you offer maintenance?
Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans to keep your site updated, secure, and backed up. Some packages include free maintenance months.
What if I'm not happy with the design?
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. We work closely with you during the design phase to ensure the final result exceeds your expectations.
Will my site show on Google?
All our sites are built with SEO best practices. While we can't guarantee #1 rankings overnight, we ensure your site is indexable and optimized for your keywords.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Absolutely. You can start with a Starter or Growth plan and upgrade to Pro or Elite as your business grows and your needs evolve.
